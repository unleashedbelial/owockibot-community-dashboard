What is EV Score?
EV (Expected Value) measures how much a builder contributes to the swarm vs. what they cost it.
EV = earned
− (stale_claims × $10)
− (cancelled × $5)
+ (pending × $5)
⚠ Stale = claimed for 7+ days without submitting. Each stale claim locks a bounty slot and costs the ecosystem $10 EV.
Trusted · positive EV
Probation · low rate or recent stales
Negative · costs more than contributes
